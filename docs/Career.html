I am currently employed at the Church of Jesus Christ of latter-Day Saints Global Support Department (GSD) as a support tech-specialist for North America and all of South American, both Spanish and Portuguese speaking countries. I have been a tech-specialist for 1.5 years, although I have been working at the GSD for 3.5 years. The first 2 years I was working as a tech-generalist. The details of a tech-generalist and a tech-specialistare detailed below.

Tech-specialist

 Review and correct 100-150 escalated tickets a week.

 Give new hire trainings every 1-2 months.

 Take and resolve 15-25 customer issues daily.

 Sit with and mentor new hires on phone calls.

 Answer technical questions from other employees.

 Carry out special assignments from supervisors.

 All the responsibilities of a generalist.

Tech-generalist

 Troubleshoot customer account and system issues.

 Resolve or handle 15-25 tickets per day.

 Resolve customer issues in a timely manner.

 Protect confidential data.

 Manage funds, make transfers, keep financial records.

 Communicate across departments to resolve caller issues.

 Use and learn customer service systems.

 Use English, Spanish, and Portuguese daily to help in North and South America.
